Meet the Curator/ Author of OysterFunKids

A Veteran Academician, Nirmala Prabhu is a Child-Life Transformation Coach, Teachers' Trainer and Author of several varied Academic Textbooks. She has curated 'Find the Budding Novelist in You’ to help metamorphosing Primary and Middle-schoolers in language acquisition and how to be a Novelist.

Her training expertise is highly regarded, As she has trained more than 5000 teachers across the country.

Nirmala's commitment to education has won her several awards like Human Rights Millennium Award, Criticspace Literary Best Indian Author Award. She has been placed among the top 1% teachers globally, in the International Teachers' Olympiad 2023 by Suraasa.
